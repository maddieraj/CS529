White Hat Visualization of Slate Gun Deaths



The map below shows the number of murder victims across the US. The overall victims is loaded onto a chloropleth map, ranging from light to darker shade, the latter indicating higher number of deaths.
Hovering on any state will give the breakdown of deaths for each state based on the gender.
Clicking on any state will show the list of deaths in each cities for that state in the table for various age groups.
