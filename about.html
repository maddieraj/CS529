Black Hat -

For the black hat visualization, I did the plotting of the overall distribution of deaths as points to mislead the visualization.
The map does not tell us about the overall death in a state as we cannot possibly count the number of dots.
Also, using the same color for both the genders does not provide us information about their split in number of victims.
Visually, if you look at the map, there seems to be more number of points in the east coast, so I chose a misleading title to make it black hat visualization.

White -

The white hat visualization is improved with chloropleth map with gradient coloring ranging from light to dark blue to indicate the number of victims..
Lighter shade has less number of death and increases with saturation.
Hovering over the map will let us know about the overall death as well as the deaths based on the gender.
Additionally, clicking on a state will display a break down with city wise deaths segregating them based on their age.
