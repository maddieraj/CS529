About this project

Although this map shows the distributions of the death across the map for each state, it is deceptive in following ways:
First, the higher number of victims are in California, but looking at this map, it misleads us into thinking the more number of deaths in somewhere in east coast.
Second, female and male markers have the same color which makes it hard to distinguish between them.
Third, merely plotting the points does not tell us anything about the overall death in that state.
Finally, the title is a bit misleading as we have no information on the total number of deaths to prove it.
